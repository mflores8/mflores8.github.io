Monica Flores is a junior at the University of Florida pursuing her Bachelors of Science in Public Relations. Following undergraduate, she plans to attend graduate school and work in a firm. Her long term career goal is to work in the nonprofit sector. Her interests include social media, branding and internal communication strategies.

During her college career, Monica has also worked at Baby Gator Child Development and Research Center. She is passionate about eliciting the fullest potential of each child and shaping them into leaders for the future.

A native of Pensacola, FL, Monica enjoys spending her time on the beach with family and friends and indulging in the exquisite local seafood.

Be sure to follow her on Twitter, add her on LinkedIn and read her blog “Coffee Talks,” where she discusses trends in public relations and communication as well as advice for college students with similar passions.
